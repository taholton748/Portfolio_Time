About Me

Hello! I'm Tyler. I'm an aspiring web developer based in Colorado, but originally from the great state of North Carolina. I'm developing my skills in the programming industry, as seen it's not going anywhere soon, with hopes of making a career for myself in web development and seeing the world. I love to travel and am excited to start a career in which that may be more realistic. Being able to pursue your career no matter where you are on this planet is a possibility I am eager to experience. So much to code. So many places to go.
